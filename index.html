About the Founder

Aravind Paraniraja, founder of Aegan Analytics, is a seasoned Power BI Developer and automation expert with 12+ years of experience in transforming business processes through insightful dashboards and efficient workflows.

Starting his journey with MS Access and VBA, Aravind mastered tools like SQL, Power BI, Power Automate, and Azure Data Factory. He has worked with clients across 3 key industries, delivering enterprise-grade analytics and saving over 4.5 FTEs through smart automation.

As the visionary behind Aegan Analytics, Aravind aims to empower startups and enterprises with actionable intelligence, seamless automation, and meaningful digital transformation.
