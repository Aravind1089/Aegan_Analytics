About Aegan Analytics & Automation

Welcome to Aegan Analytics & Automation, founded by a seasoned Data Professional with over 12 years of experience in data platforms, reporting automation, and business intelligence. My journey began with hands-on work in MS Access, Excel VBA, and SQL, evolving into a specialized career as a Power BI Developer.

For the past 6 years, I’ve led impactful BI initiatives across industries, streamlining reporting processes, driving insights, and enabling data-driven decision-making. At Accenture, I currently lead a team of developers, handling 12+ contracts and automating 250+ reports using Power BI, Power Automate, and Power Apps.

My strengths lie in zero-touch automation, dashboard design, stakeholder collaboration, and performance optimization. I am also skilled in prompt engineering and integrating AI tools like Copilot to enhance analytics and incident management.

Passionate about continuous learning, I am expanding my expertise into Python, Azure Databricks, and machine learning to bring predictive capabilities to service management data. My long-term vision is to build my own analytics consulting brand—Aegan Analytics & Automation—to solve real-world business problems using intelligent automation and clean data storytelling.
